About Me

I'm Raghav, an IIT Roorkee'24 graduate. A Flutter engineer and founder based in India. I've been building Flutter apps since 2020. Six years, 15+ products, domains ranging from healthtech and edtech to AI, consumer tech, e-commerce, and event tech. What's stayed constant across all of them is how I approach the work. My three non-negotiables in mobile development: clean design with zero UX friction, architecture documented from day one, and speed - not shortcuts, but the speed that comes from knowing exactly what you're building before you build it.
